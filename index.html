Hi! I'm Angela

(Full name Maria Fe Angela Comia Boco)

Born in Italy, with Filipino origins. I grew up in Rome where I studied Agricultural and Industrial Biotechnology. I became fascinated by the endless applications that modern biotech tools could have in our world. So I decided to move to Bologna and continue my education with a master's degree in Horticultural Sciences. During this time I also spent 6 months in Munich, to add more biotech subjects to my curriculum. After my graduation, I moved to Spain for a post-graduation internship. This is the time I had to think and decided on whether I wanted to go down the research path. After some thinking, I realized that while I wanted to remain into the world of science, I didn't want to do so by becoming a lab rat. So when given the opportunity, I packed my things and moved to Poland to work in academic publishing. On the side, I finally decided to pursue my curiosity towards coding and enrolled in the SheCodes Workshops.
